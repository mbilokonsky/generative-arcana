# Majors — Journey

Derive the 22 major beats from a story the theme already tells, rather than from number theory. Best when the theme contains a strong native arc (a magnum opus, a hero's journey, a life cycle, a campaign with stages).

No major-rank layer — generate the 22 cards directly, integrated per `references/integration.md` with `major_arcana.visual_style` (declared) and each slot's `station_slug` (sublimated — already fixed by the major walk).

## Procedure

1. **Name the arc.** The theme's native story as a single trajectory from before-the-beginning to completion. This is `major_arcana.story`.

2. **Find the two anchors.** Card 0 (precondition / pure potential / before-the-first-step) and Card 1 (the first act of agency, the operator that opens the rest). They ground the sequence.

3. **Segment into 22 beats.** Carve the arc into 22 archetypal moments; pressure-test for *distinctness* (the common failure is two beats that are the same beat). The three-act shape (awareness → integration → transcendence) catches a saggy middle; honor it or not.

4. **Let station and number seed the beats.** Each slot's station is fixed (`major_number mod N`) — where a station has landed, let it constrain the beat (a beat on a "low tide / Ebb"-style station should be *receding*, not cresting). And the slot's prime/composite character is derivable and latent (`references/numeric_axis.md`): a prime-numbered beat may read as a more *irreducible* moment of the arc even though you didn't build it by factorization. Lean on these where they clarify; ignore where they don't.

5. **Build the cards** — integrate each per `references/integration.md`. Surface a station only where a beat plainly wears it.

## Quality

- 22 distinct beats, no two collapsible into one.
- Reads as a single story end to end, not a themed list.
- `major_arcana.visual_style` could contain the four suit styles without being indistinguishable from any one.
