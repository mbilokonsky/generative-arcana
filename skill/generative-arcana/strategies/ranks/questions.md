# Ranks ‚Äî Questions

Define the 14 minor ranks (10 numbered + 4 face) as abstract frameworks that refract through every suit. Each numbered rank poses a **question** the suit answers, so all four cards of a rank share a question while differing by suit ‚Äî deep structural parallax across the row. The default rank method.

Each rank is a `Rank` per `references/schema.md`: `description` (the framework/abstraction), `question` (the question itself), `meaning` palette (3‚Äì6 upright + chiral inverted), `visual_content`.

## Numbered ranks (Ace‚ÄìTen)

Each numbered rank's `question` uses a **literal `{suit}` placeholder** ‚Äî store it with the placeholder intact (e.g. `"Under what conditions does {suit} emerge?"`) so a consumer substitutes the suit name cleanly at display time. Reuse or adapt:

- **Ace** ‚Äî Under what conditions does {suit} emerge?
- **Two** ‚Äî What is {suit}'s relationship to duality?
- **Three** ‚Äî How does {suit} relate to systemic complexity?
- **Four** ‚Äî What stabilizes {suit}?
- **Five** ‚Äî What emerges from {suit} under pressure?
- **Six** ‚Äî What threatens {suit}?
- **Seven** ‚Äî How does {suit} factionalize or decompose?
- **Eight** ‚Äî What is a stable {suit} capable of?
- **Nine** ‚Äî What is the teleology of {suit}?
- **Ten** ‚Äî What does it mean for {suit} to complete?

For each: name the question, give the palette, describe `visual_content` general enough to instantiate per suit yet recognizable across suits. The Ace‚ÜíTen progression should read as a coherent ascent.

The **fourth axis is latent here too** (`references/numeric_axis.md`): a prime-numbered rank (2, 3, 5, 7) tends to pose a more *irreducible* question, a composite (4, 6, 8, 9, 10) a more *derived* one ‚Äî even though you're not structuring by factorization. Let that tint the question's framing where it helps; ignore it otherwise. (Face ranks 11‚Äì14 carry numeric character too: 11, 13 prime; 12, 14 composite.)

Present the ten numbered ranks for feedback before the face ranks.

## Face ranks (11‚Äì14)

Four roles ‚Äî archetypes from the major arcana projected through suit space. Requirements:
- **Distinct initials** (for the glyph; set `symbol` to the initial).
- **No collisions** with suit names or major-arcana names.
- **Encoded progression** 11‚Üí14: hierarchy (Student‚ÜíWarrior‚ÜíNurturing Ruler‚ÜíCommanding Ruler), cycle (Spring‚ÜíSummer‚ÜíAutumn‚ÜíWinter), composition (Element‚ÜíCompound‚ÜíMixture‚ÜíSynthesis), development (Observation‚ÜíExperimentation‚ÜíIntegration‚ÜíMastery). Hierarchical, cyclic, rhizomatic, or distributed ‚Äî between them they should capture some aspect of the major arcana and show how it applies suit by suit.

Propose a progression fitting the deck's concepts; present for feedback.
